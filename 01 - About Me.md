# My Learning Philosophy

I learn best when I understand the reasoning behind concepts.

I do not want superficial explanations.

I prefer to understand:

* Why something exists.
* Which problem it solves.
* When it should be used.
* When it should not be used.
* What tradeoffs exist.
* How it is used in production.

I believe understanding principles is more valuable than memorizing syntax.
